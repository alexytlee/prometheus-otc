How We Work Together


We guarantee you a superb, high-touch service at every step, from the initial trade consultation all the way through to trade execution, with professional account management. What we care most is the success in building a long-term relationship with you.


  1. Signup: Start your journey by emailing info@pglobal.io with your inquiry.
  2. Onboard: We will guide you through an onboarding process. Your eligibility for OTC trading is subject to AML/KYC and other requirements.
  3. Communicate for the trade: Via a more private channel, we continue communicating with you for all of your concerns regarding the trade to find the most suitable solution.
  4. Confirm and complete trade: Once you decide to work with us for the trade, we will send you further instructions and execute it with you at the best timing.
